# Feature Ideas

A running list of ideas for future HBBC website features. Not commitments —
just things worth considering. Move an idea to `TODO.md` when it's ready to
be worked on.

## Matchday & VfB

- **Tippspiel (score prediction game)** — members predict VfB match scores
  before kickoff, leaderboard tracks points over the season. Pairs naturally
  with the existing VfB match data on `/events`.
- **Fahrgemeinschaften (carpooling board)** — members offer/request rides to
  away games, tied to a specific upcoming match.
- **Live matchday chat or comment thread** — a lightweight per-match
  discussion thread that opens up around kickoff.
- **Attendance RSVP with headcount** — "Wer kommt mit?" button on Fanclub
  events/away-game trips, so organizers know numbers in advance.

## Community & Members

- **Member of the month / spotlight** — a small featured card on the home
  page or Mitglieder page highlighting a member (with their own words),
  rotated periodically.
- **Guestbook / shoutout wall** — a lightweight public wall for well-wishes,
  matchday memories, or shoutouts, separate from testimonials.
- **Birthday shoutouts** — opt-in birthday field on profile, small banner on
  the day (admins/members can already see joined-date, this extends it).
- **Achievements/badges** — small non-serious gamification (e.g. "5 Jahre
  dabei", "Erstes Auswärtsspiel") shown on a member's profile.

## Gallery & Content

- **Photo-of-the-month voting** — members vote on their favorite photo from
  the current month's approved uploads; winner gets featured.
- **Event-linked albums** — let an album optionally reference a specific
  fanclub event or VfB match, so galleries organize themselves around what
  actually happened.

## Engagement & Utility

- **VfB trivia/quiz** — a small quiz page about club history or the fanclub
  itself, purely for fun.
- **Anniversary/history timeline** — a scrollable timeline of the fanclub's
  own history (founding, milestones, notable trips).
- **PWA / installable app** — add a manifest + service worker so the site
  can be added to a phone home screen with an app icon, useful for members
  checking match times on the go.
- **English toggle** — a lightweight i18n pass for non-German-speaking VfB
  fans or guests, at least on the public-facing pages.
- **WhatsApp/Telegram quick-link** — a visible link to the fanclub's group
  chat for members who want faster, informal coordination than the website.

## Admin/CMS

- **Newsletter scheduling** — compose now, send later at a chosen date/time,
  building on the existing newsletter composer.
- **Simple analytics dashboard** — page-view / signup counts for admins,
  no third-party tracker, just server-side counters.
